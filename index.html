Vimseltinib (Romvimza) Approved for Tenosynovial Giant Cell Tumor

In early 2025, the FDA approved Vimseltinib (brand name Romvizma), a targeted CSFIR kinase inhibitor for adults with tenosynovial giant cell tumor (TGCT) who are not eligible for surgery. Unlike traditional therapies, Vimseltinib interrupts key signaling pathways in tumor-associated macrophages.
